Kolkata Munchies – The Most Desirable

Manish MandalJuly 7, 2019

So Food Lovers Out There, Let’s Get Started

Kolkata and the Food it offers – sounds much more like a love story. Kolkata is like food heaven for every food lover out there especially the street food or snacks it offers. Starting from the top-notch restaurants to the street stalls, they all have finger-licking foods. The most inexpensive food paradise, if I may say so. Here is a list of top favorite munchies of Kolkattans.

  • Phuchka

Hey Phuchka! You always need to be at the top of the list. You, people, may call it as golgappe, panipuri, pakodi but Kolkattans gets the feelings when they call it Phuchka, the crispiness, and the aalu filling with varieties of chutneys and dahi… It makes it the most desirable.

  • Chow Mein

Here we are not talking about Chinese Hakka noodles or the Meifoons but the simple roadside chow mein. Oh! It is so mouth-watering. Be it egg, chicken, mixed or simple veg chow mein, their aroma, and their texture… You cannot miss.

  • Kathi Roll

Can we miss out on the rolls while talking about the chow mein? Of course not. No. Never. Crunchy paratha with either egg or chicken or paneer or keema whatever you wish, get it right in this city.

  • Jhal Muri

The quickest savior when running late. Crispy muri mixed with peanuts, aalu, onion, chilli and all kind of spices, a very personal vintage and yes, Kolkattans favorite. Have it with chai or any kind of telebhaja (another Kolkata favorite deep-fried snack) the taste is mesmerizing.

  • Ghugni

Yet another quick meal and a much loved go-to snack. Have it alone or with bread or luchi (puri) it always tastes delicious. Ghugni topped with emmly, chutney, peanuts, and onions are served on the typical saal pata… Ahaan! Simply delightful it is.

  • Kachori Sabzi

Ahhh! Just the name makes me hungry. It is a very famous and mouth-watering breakfast of Kolkattans. Small kachori with aalu or dal is absolutely soul-filling.
